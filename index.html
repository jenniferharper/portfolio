Career
Highlights

From August 2017 to December 2021 I worked as a Web Designer at Spotzer Digital. Aside from creating engaging websites on Wordpress, Webflow, Duda, and Mono platforms for SMB's across Europe, America and Australia, I worked on a few major Spotzer projects as lead designer. You can see my favourite two projects below.

Focus Project One

In 2019 my logo and identity concept was chosen for our client Wix Builders. Providing customers with the opportunity to have professional designers and copywriters build their website on the Wix platform, Wix Builders required a friendly, approachable, professional brand identity. In addition to building their website I was involved in template design , and offshore vendor training.

Focus Project Two
